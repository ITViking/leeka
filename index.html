The

Leakage Detector

is an instrument that

is designed to:

  • Give real time process control
  • Detect small streams or droplet of liquid
  • Be installed after a valve
  • Send an alarm if a valve is leaking

The Leakage Detector is based on a patented leakage detection technology

The Leakage Detector is a standalone instrument

The Leakage Detector technology can also be build into a valve

Designed to improve process control

Control and minimize the risk of:

  • Contamination of end product and cross contamination
  • Spillage and environmental damage

Minimizing cost:

  • Preventive maintenance
  • Cost due to spillage
